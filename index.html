Privacy Policy v1.0 — Faux Marketplace

Faux Marketplace (“the App”) is a demo marketplace experience. The App does not collect, store, sell, or share any user data of any kind.

Data Collection

  • No personal data is collected.
  • No payment data is collected.
  • No analytics or tracking data is collected.
  • No location data is collected.
  • No identifiers are collected (such as device ID, advertising ID, or IP address).

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the support page listed on the App Store.
